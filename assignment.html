    more difficult to deal with. While this is old news, most programmers don't know or don't

  • Defining Syntactic Sugar I sometimes hear arguments about what is or is not syntactic

  • Class-based Programming as Syntactic Sugar. Most of the time, "Object Oriented"
    actually mean classes. Classes are nothing more than syntactic sugar, at least in statically

    understand classes better.
